user@about

I am a Research Scientist in the AI/ML group at Apple, working on intelligent user interfaces. I received my Ph.D. from Harvard, where I was advised by Prof. Elena Glassman at the Variation Lab. My research lies in the intersection of Human-Computer Interaction, Artificial Intelligence, and Software Engineering.

Prior to this, I was a Master's Student at UCSD, where I worked with Prof. Philip Guo at the UCSD Design Lab. I was also fortunate to work in Microsoft Research India as a Research Fellow for two years, where I worked on projects related to information retrieval, developer tools, and programming accessibility.
